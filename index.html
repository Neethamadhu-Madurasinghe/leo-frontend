Leo Club of University of Colombo

The Leo Club of University of Colombo is an esteemed and distinguished social service organization, widely regarded for its unwavering commitment to cultivating leadership skills among its members. Affiliated with the esteemed youth program of Lions Clubs International, and belonging to the esteemed Leo District 306C1, this club stands as a testament to the highest standards of excellence and accomplishment. With its inception dating back to 2015, the Leo Club of University of Colombo has established itself as a beacon of leadership and service, setting a standard of unparalleled excellence in the field of social service.
